# Privacy Policy — Neon Asteroids

**Last updated:** March 4, 2026

## Introduction

Neon Asteroids ("the Game") is developed by Kevin Delfour / Delfour.co. This privacy policy explains how the Game handles your data.

## Data Collection

**Neon Asteroids does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The Game is fully offline and does not require an internet connection to function.

## Local Storage

The Game uses local device storage (SharedPreferences) solely to save:

- **Leaderboard scores** — Your top 10 high scores are stored locally on your device only.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

The Game does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services.

## Children's Privacy

The Game does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
