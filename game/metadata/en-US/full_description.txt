Neon Asteroids is a neon-styled arcade shooter inspired by the classic Asteroids.

Pilot your spaceship through asteroid fields, face waves of UFO enemies, and collect power-ups to survive as long as possible.

Features:
• Smooth touch controls with joystick and action buttons
• Progressive wave system with asteroids and space debris
• UFO enemies: Scouts (wave 3+) and Hunters (wave 6+)
• Power-ups: shield, multi-shot, slow motion
• Dash to dodge dangers
• Combo system to maximize your score
• Local Top 10 leaderboard
• Animated galactic background with Antennae nebula
• Realistic space debris: Starlink, ISS/MIR, Tesla Roadster + Starman
• Ambient synth music with reactive volume
• 14 sound effects for full arcade immersion
• Sound ON/OFF toggle in pause menu
